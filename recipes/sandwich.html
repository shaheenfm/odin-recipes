Juicy Chicken Sandwich

Description

These Pesto Grilled Cheese Sandwiches are a perfect lunch and a cheap and fulfilling meal too! It takes only 5 minutes to prep and 10 minutes to cook, and the ingredients are very cheap and can be found in any kitchen. This is a must try for pesto lovers!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Italian bread
  • 1 tablespoon softened butter, divided
  • 1 tablespoon prepared pesto sauce, divided
  • 1 slice provolone cheese
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 1 slice American cheese
