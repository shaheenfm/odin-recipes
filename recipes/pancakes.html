Fluffy Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe for blueberry pancakes made with a dash of grounded flax-seed for taste. It needs very little prep time (just 5 minutes!) and cooks in the same time! One batch serves 4 and the pancakes are high in protein as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix
  • 1/2 cup flax seed meal
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh or thawed frozen blueberries

Steps:
  1. Set a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together the pancake mix and flax seed meal. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk and eggs. Pour the liquid into the dry and stir until moistened.
  3. Spoon 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the hot skillet. Sprinkle with as many blueberries as desired. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.
